Must Know Terms

Step 1 : Understand the following terms

Category Unique ID (CUI) :

It refers to the ID of the category of products (eg : 500ML, 1L, 2L Bottles etc) that your firm manufactures. Don't have CUI's for your products? Click the button to generate !!

Category Production Quantity (CPQ) :

It refers to the number of plastic products of a particular category that are being produced on a particular day.

Unit Plastic Quantity (UPQ) :

It refers to the amount of plastic used in manufacturing each of the plastic products of a paticular category.

Date Of Manufacture (DOM) :

It refers to the date on which the plastic products have been manufactured.
